KEYWORD GLOSSARY

Team: Grants stats to other friendly cards that share its type at start of battle.

Anger: Grants Attack to all allies when this card dies.

Honor: Grants HP to all allies when this card dies.

Cheap: While in squad, shop cards cost less (min 0).

Twin: Only requires 1 duplicate instead of 2 to merge and level up.

Extra: Spawns a token (stats based on effect) when this card dies.

Weak: Permanently reduces stats of a random enemy when this card dies.

Solo: Gains stats at start of battle if it's the only one of its type in squad.
